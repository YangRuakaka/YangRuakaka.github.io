About

I am currently a final-year undergraduate student at Tongji University. Starting from Fall 2026, I will start my PhD journey at University of Notre Dame, advised by Prof. Toby Li.

My research interests are generally around evaluating and improving the capabilities of AI for human-AI collaboration. More recently, I am working on benchmarking and training proactive MLLMs to better assist and collaborate with humans.

Research Focus

Human-AI Alignment

Designing systems that help users interpret, adapt to, and meaningfully engage with AI behavior, ensuring models align with human intentions and values.

Agent Evaluation

Developing process-oriented evaluation frameworks that move beyond task accuracy to capture how LLM agents' reasoning aligns with human cognitive processes.

Human-AI Collaboration

Building interactive interfaces that enable users to articulate goals, steer model behavior, and collaborate with AI systems effectively.
